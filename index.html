Journal #1: Two Days Ago

Can I Sleep? 2021-04-05

Two days ago I wanted to sleep for the whole day. After a challenging Winter quarter, I just wanted to sleep. I felt that the break was too short to regain any type of energy. I decided to go ahead and take a nap and push back some of the assignments that needed to be done. Although they were pushed back, it was not a problem at all as it is the beginning of the quarter and there is not much to do. These past two weeks of the Spring quarter I have just been sleeping most of the time.

Fortunately, I was able to complete my list of assignments, which were:

  • Watch BILD 3 lecture
  • Turn in HW #0 for CSE 101
  • Go over practice problems for CSE 101
  • Create plan for the following day

However, I did pushback somethings which were the lowlights of the day:

  • Getting somewhat behind
  • Pushing back things makes me even more tired

Here is the list in order that needs to be done:

  1. Find a group for CSE 101
  2. Go to office hours for CSE 101
  3. Prepare for the upcoming CSE 101 quiz
In order to get energized for the quarter, I looked through some images of San Diego, which always makes me smile a bit.

Journal #2: One Day Ago

Ghost In My House? 2021-04-06

Yesterday I was pretty much alone at my house. I was finishing up some assignments for this Spring quarter. At around 9PM I heard some knocking in my living room. Didn't think of it too much. A few minutes go by and I hear it again. I thought there was someone outside, so I go out and take a look around the house. Nothing. I go back to my room and hear another sound. It sounded like someone was hitting one of the pictures on the wall with a coin. Dink, Dink, Dink.

This was one of the many things that has happened. Here is a list of my observations:

  • Coin hitting a picture
  • Someone dancing on my desk at 3AM
  • Something punching the couch right next to me (very rare)
  • Weird whispers in my ear while I am sleeping telling me to avoid a trolley bridge by the 805
  • Objects slowly moving seemingly to test my view

But there are some lowlights (I think):

  • Probably a cat?
  • Could be the wooden beams just being old
  • Very small earthquakes

Here is the list in order that I could do to identify this stuff:

  1. Setup a camera in the living room
  2. Edit it to skip to the good parts
  3. Analyze to investigate
This is what the ghost probably wants me to do

Journal #3: Today-ago

When will School End? 2021-04-07

Today I want to reflect on my academic experiences. I have been in school for a long time. I just want to get my bachelors and get out. I am so close, too. I spent four years at a community college due to frequent major changes. Each year there felt like a lost year. I feel great being at UCSD and I am doing well so far. However, I hope I can get closure some day and move on into the real world doing things I want.

Here are some things that I have accomplished:

  • Find a major that I can see myself doing
  • Keep in touch with some professors
  • Get into UCSD
  • Get a 4.0 in at least one of the quarters
  • Feel that I am making progress

But there are some lowlights where I have not accomplished:

  • Build a strong resume
  • Build a network of people
  • Build a personal brand

Here is the list in order that I could do to accomplish the previous list:

  1. Attend a resume workshop
  2. Talk to professors about industry
  3. Look at successful people and their branding
